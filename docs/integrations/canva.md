# Canva

Canva connects your design workspace to Starfish. Create and edit designs, presentations, social media graphics, and marketing materials using natural language -- without opening the Canva editor.


## How to Connect

1. Open **Settings > Integrations** in Starfish.
2. Find **Canva** and click **Connect**.
3. A browser window will open to Canva's authorization page.
4. Sign in to your Canva account and approve the connection.
5. You'll be redirected back to Starfish. The integration status will show **Connected**.

## What You Can Do

- **Create Designs** -- Generate new designs from text descriptions (social posts, banners, flyers, etc.)
- **Edit Designs** -- Modify existing Canva designs (update text, swap images, adjust layouts)
- **Presentations** -- Create and edit slide decks
- **Marketing Materials** -- Generate branded marketing assets
- **Templates** -- Work with Canva's template library
- **Export** -- Download designs in various formats

## Example Prompts

- "Create an Instagram post announcing our summer sale with a tropical theme"
- "Design a presentation deck for our Q2 investor update with 8 slides"
- "Update the header text on my latest Facebook banner to say 'Now Hiring'"
- "Create a LinkedIn carousel about our 5 core values"

## Notes

- Canva uses OAuth PKCE for authentication. The connection is handled through a secure browser popup.
- The AI can create designs using your Canva account's available templates, brand kit, and assets.
- For best results, describe the design you want in detail -- include colors, style, dimensions, and intended platform.
