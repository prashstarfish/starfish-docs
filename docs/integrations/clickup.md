# ClickUp

ClickUp connects your project management workspace to Starfish. Create and manage tasks, track time, organize projects, and collaborate on docs -- all through your AI workspace.


## How to Connect

1. Open **Settings > Integrations** in Starfish.
2. Find **ClickUp** and click **Connect**.
3. A browser window will open to ClickUp's authorization page.
4. Sign in to your ClickUp account and approve the connection.
5. You'll be redirected back to Starfish. The integration status will show **Connected**.

## What You Can Do

- **Tasks** -- Create, update, delete, search, and filter tasks across your workspace
- **Projects** -- Browse your workspace hierarchy (spaces, folders, lists)
- **Docs** -- Create documents and pages, read and update content
- **Time Tracking** -- Start/stop timers, log time entries, view time reports
- **Comments** -- Add comments to tasks, read threaded discussions
- **Tags** -- Add and remove tags on tasks
- **Dependencies** -- Set task dependencies and linked relationships
- **Reminders** -- Create, update, and search reminders
- **Members** -- Look up workspace members by name
- **Chat** -- Read and send messages in ClickUp Chat channels

## Example Prompts

- "Create a task in the 'Sprint 12' list called 'Fix login redirect bug' and assign it to Sarah"
- "What tasks are due this week in the Engineering space?"
- "Log 2 hours of time on task #abc123 for today"
- "Show me all tasks tagged 'urgent' that are overdue"
- "Create a doc in the Product space with our meeting notes from today"

## Notes

- ClickUp uses direct OAuth for authentication. No tokens or manual setup required.
- The AI has access to everything your ClickUp account can see. Use ClickUp's built-in permissions to control what data is available.
- Task filters support custom fields, statuses, assignees, tags, due dates, and more.
